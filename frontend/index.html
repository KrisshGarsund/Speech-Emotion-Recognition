How It Works

Powered by deep learning and advanced audio processing

Audio Input

Upload pre-recorded audio files or record live speech directly through your microphone with real-time visualization.

Feature Extraction

Extracts 40 MFCC (Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients) features that capture the unique acoustic characteristics of emotions.

CNN+LSTM Network

Hybrid deep learning architecture combining Convolutional Neural Networks for pattern recognition with LSTM for temporal modeling.

Deep Learning Analysis

Trained on 1,440 professional audio samples achieving 78% accuracy through systematic hyperparameter optimization.

8 Emotions Detected

Accurately classifies Happy, Sad, Angry, Fearful, Calm, Surprised, Disgust, and Neutral emotions with confidence scores.

Confidence Metrics

Real-World Applications
